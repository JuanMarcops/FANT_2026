On 4 September 2026, researchers in the archaeology of Africa will gather in Frankfurt. The aim is to maintain the network established through the SPP "Entangled Africa", to foster new connections, and to promote research in African archaeology. Contributions are planned in two formats: presentations for sharing research projects and findings, and roundtables where a challenge or open question from one's own field is introduced in a brief impulse talk before being discussed collectively.
